Contact addresses

Getting in touch about a site under .user.srcf.net or .soc.srcf.net?

The SRCF hosts many user websites on its systems, and we provide built-in domains of the form CRSid.user.srcf.net or acronym.soc.srcf.net.

The SRCF itself is not immediately responsible for content under these domains.

In the first instance, you should direct queries to the owners of the relevant account as follows:

  • Websites like spqr2.user.srcf.net: emails to spqr2@srcf.net.
  • Websites like cuxs.soc.srcf.net: emails to cuxs-webmaster@srcf.net.

For communications relating to infringing copyrighted material or other abuse, please also CC soc-srcf-admin@lists.cam.ac.uk.

We also host websites on custom domains, which do not end in .srcf.net. Please contact the system administrators at soc-srcf-admin@lists.cam.ac.uk if you're getting in touch about a website hosted by us under a custom domain and are unsure how to reach the site's owners.
