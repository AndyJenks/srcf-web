Getting involved

The SRCF is entirely volunteer-run so we're glad you're here! There are many ways to get involved in the SRCF and none of them require any prerequisite level of skill. You might find it helpful just to be immersed in our community, as we can give you the tools and knowledge you need to gain sysadmin skils.

  • Participate in community discussion

    • This is where members of the SRCF community come to chat, hang out and discuss ideas.

    We are dedicated to providing a safe, inclusive, welcoming, harassment-free experience for members of the SRCF community. Note that participation in these platforms is subject to our code of conduct.

    • or use Internet Relay Chat over the webclient, or via a dedicated client on irc.srcf.net #hackday

    You can join any platform as they are all bridged :)

  • Contribute to our software

    • Most components of our operation are public on GitHub. Feel free to open an issue, or better yet, a pull request with your proposed changes.

  • Give us feedback
    • We rely on user feedback to improve and grow. It takes only two minutes to help us out.
