Exceptional Items

User accounts policy

  • We should be prepared to have to cancel graduated users if the UIS’ opinion changes.
  • We will receive a lot of @cam auto responses saying “my new email address is…”.
    • The majority of those will not want to keep those accounts.
    • Automatically changing the email addresses on their accounts has DPA issues.
    • This would introduce a lot of work.
    • We should treat any bounce—including the auto-response—as “un-contactable” and therefore cancel them. This should be explicit in the ToS.
  • We could warn people a few weeks before graduation with instructions on how to keep your account (i.e., changing the email address).
  • We should issue an email in September from a special email address that lets us collect bounces for automated cancelling.
  • Action Sysadmins: draft new terms of service. The sysadmins have the permission of the committee to proceed without review at another committee meeting.
  • We should warn people in advance of the new terms of service, before they go into effect, via email.
