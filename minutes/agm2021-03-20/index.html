SRCF AGM 2021

Meeting details

The 2021 Annual General Meeting of the Student-Run Computing Facility will be held during the final week of Lent term.

The meeting will commence at 14:00 GMT on Saturday 20 March 2021.

You will be able to join the AGM via Timeout at https://timeout.srcf.net/agm.

Standing Agenda Items

  • Executive annual reports
    • Chair's report
    • Treasurer's report
    • Sysadmins' report
  • Consideration of received motions, if any
  • Election procedure
    • Election candidates' speeches
    • Voting (procedure TBD)

Call for Submissions

If you wish to stand for election to the committee, take a look at the dedicated section below.

If you have a motion for the AGM, please contact the Secretary at least three days in advance of the meeting date with details of the motion. Examples of types of motion are resolutions to adopt a policy, mandates to the SRCF committee, or constitutional amendments.

Committee Elections

The AGM will include the election of the SRCF Committee for 2021-22.

Regardless of any prior involvement in the SRCF, we encourage you to stand for a committee position if you care about championing free and accessible computing for everyone at Cambridge

Rules on the election of the SRCF Committee are contained in Clause 5 of the constitution and the most important points are reproduced below:

  • "Nominations must be submitted to the Secretary at least three days before the AGM (in the absence of other nominations for any post, nominations shall be accepted at the AGM for that post), either by email or on paper, to reach the Secretary by that deadline signed by proposer, seconder and nominee."
  • "Any member of the society may be nominated for any Committee post (a proposer and seconder are needed). The Chair of the AGM shall appoint two tellers at the AGM."

If you are interested in running for any of the above positions, but you want to know more about what is involved, you might want to read our page about roles in the SRCF. Do not let the proposer and seconder requirement discourage you! If you send in a short manifesto/blurb explaining your motivation for standing for the position, the committee will be happy to propose/second you.

Other Notes

  • The Secretary can be contacted at secretary@srcf.net.
  • If you cannot make the AGM, but still want to tell us something/help/contribute, please contact the committee at committee@srcf.net.
  • There are other ways to get involved in the SRCF. Interested sysadmins and volunteers are always needed. Contact sysadmins@srcf.net to express your interest -- no prior involvement required.
