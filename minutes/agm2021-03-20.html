Amendments

JJ’s ammendment

Proposed by: jjag3

Purpose: remove the use of gender-specific pronouns from the Constitution.

Motivation

The current pronouns have been introduced over various amendments. This amendment would make our usage both
more consistent and more inclusive. There is no intention to change the role of Senior Treasurer, as specified in
clause 8.

Changes

Clause 6: The Committee shall consist of Chair, Secretary, Junior Treasurer, and PR officer. It shall meet at least once per term, and may invite any other persons to attend Committee meetings as it sees fit. The Senior Treasurer shall always be invited to attend Committee meetings if they wish to.

Clause 7: In addition there shall be a Senior Treasurer, who shall be a resident member of the Senate or other person approved by the Junior Proctor. The Senior Treasurer shall make arrangements for the Society’s Accounts to be
properly audited, either by themselves, or by some other person approved under University Ordinances. The Senior Treasurer shall not be liable for any financial debt or other obligation of the Society unless they have personally authorised such a debt in writing.
