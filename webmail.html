SRCF Webmail

Starting in October 2018, the SRCF runs two email services. Please choose which webmail service you would like.

SRCF Hades webmail

Hades is the standard SRCF email service since October 2018. If you signed up since then, or migrated your email, this is probably the one you want.

webmail.hades.srcf.net

pip webmail

Before October 2018, the only SRCF mail service was hosted on pip (our shell server). For older mailboxes, or if you chose to use pip, use this one.

webmail.pip.srcf.net
